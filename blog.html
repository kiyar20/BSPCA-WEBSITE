Valentine's Day Community Winner

Happy Valentine’s Day Botswana! Our shelter staff voted for their favourite pictures in the Valentine’s Day competition, and the winning photo showed a beautiful bond between a family, a baby, and their cat.

The competition celebrated adopted pets and the loving homes they become part of. BSPCA thanked everyone who entered and appreciated the community support for animal adoption.

Message: Adoption changes lives, both for animals and the families who welcome them.

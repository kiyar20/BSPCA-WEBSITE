Our Mandate

BSPCA aims to protect animals against cruelty, educate the public, and promote responsible animal welfare in Botswana.

The Five Freedoms of Animals

The Five Freedoms help explain the basic welfare needs every animal should have.

Freedom from Hunger & Thirst
Freedom from Discomfort
Freedom to Express Behaviour
Freedom from Fear & Distress
Freedom from Pain, Injury & Disease

Current Challenges

  • Limited shelter resources and funding
  • Stray animal population growth
  • Need for stronger public education
  • Need for improved animal welfare awareness
